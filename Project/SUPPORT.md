## Frequently Asked Questions

### Getting Started

**How do I create an account?**
Download the Aura Farm app and tap **Sign Up**. Enter your email address and choose a display name. You'll receive a verification email — confirm it to activate your account.

**Is Aura Farm free?**
Yes, completely free.

**Who is Aura Farm for?**
Aura Farm is designed for college and university students. Challenges are placed at campus and nearby locations.

---

### Challenges & Points

**How do I complete a challenge?**
Navigate to a challenge location, tap on it in the app, and submit a photo as proof. You must be physically present — the app verifies your GPS location before accepting the submission.

**Why was my submission rejected?**
Submissions are rejected if your GPS location doesn't match the challenge location. Make sure you're at the correct spot and that location permissions are enabled for Aura Farm in your phone's settings.

**Can I complete the same challenge more than once?**
No. Each challenge can only be completed once per user.

**When do new challenges appear?**
New challenges are added regularly. Check back each week for fresh ones.

**Why didn't I receive my points?**
Points are awarded immediately upon a successful submission. If your points didn't update, try pulling down to refresh your profile. If the issue persists, contact us.

### Account & Profile

**How do I change my display name?**
Go to **Settings** in the app and update your display name there.

**How do I reset my password?**
On the login screen, tap **Forgot Password** and enter your email. You'll receive a reset link.

**How do I delete my account?**
To permanently delete your account and all associated data, email us at [support@codeboxorg.com](mailto:support@codeboxorg.com) with the subject line **"Delete My Account"** and the email address tied to your account. We'll process the request within 7 business days.

### Technical Issues

**The app won't load / keeps crashing.**
Try the following steps:
1. Force-quit the app and reopen it.
2. Check your internet connection.
3. Delete and reinstall the app (your account data is saved server-side).
4. If the issue persists, email us with your device model and OS version.

**Location isn't being detected correctly.**
Make sure:
- Location permissions are set to **While Using the App** (or **Always**) in your phone's Settings → Aura Farm.
- You have a clear GPS signal (move outside if indoors).
- Your device's location services are turned on globally.

**I'm not receiving the verification email.**
Check your spam/junk folder. If it's not there, tap **Resend Verification** on the verification screen. Still nothing? Email us and we'll verify your account manually.

## Community Guidelines

To keep Aura Farm a positive experience for everyone:

- Submit **honest proof** — photos must actually show you completing the challenge.
- Be **respectful** in captions and interactions.
- Do **not** submit on behalf of someone else or fake your location.
- Do **not** post offensive, harmful, or inappropriate photos.

Violations may result in submission removal or account suspension.
